Discover the City of Nawabs

Lucknow, the capital of Uttar Pradesh, India, is a city known for its rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and culinary delights. Often called the "City of Nawabs," it boasts beautiful Mughal and colonial architecture. Key attractions include the grand Bara Imambara, the iconic Rumi Darwaza, and the bustling Hazratganj market. Lucknow is also famous for its exquisite cuisine, with dishes like Galouti Kebabs and Lucknawi Biryani. The city offers a unique blend of traditional and modern charm, making it a must-visit destination.

Top Attractions in Lucknow

Bara Imambara

A grand mosque built by Asaf-ud-Daula, a marvel of Mughal architecture.

Rumi Darwaza

Also known as the Turkish Gate, a stunning example of Awadhi architecture.

Chota Imambara

A beautiful monument that reflects the grandeur of the Nawabi era.

Hazratganj Market

A bustling shopping area with a mix of modern and traditional shops.
